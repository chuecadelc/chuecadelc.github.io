Since I joined the committee of the European Social Simulation Association [ESSA](https://essa.eu.org) in 2024, I have been actively involved in their communication strategy and event organisation. Recently, I took my involvement one step further by creatig the website for the most recent edition of the Social Simulation Festival (SocSimFest) as well as being the lead organiser of this event for the second consecutive year. I did so using WordPress and the Divi Page builder. You can find out the results here -> [SocSimFest2026](https://socsimfest.eu)
