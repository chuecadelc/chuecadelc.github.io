Guest lecturer for the Practicing Research and Working with Data in the Digital Age (SOCIO5102, Postgraduate) course, part of Digital Society MSc programme. Delivered two 2-hour lectures online (Zoom) in 2021 and in person in 2022. The topic was on "The impact of digitalization through social media platforms: Applications of social network analysis (SNA)". The slides are available [here](https://chuecadelc.github.io/IntroSNA/) See more details of the course [here](https://www.gla.ac.uk/postgraduate/taught/digitalsociety/?card=course&code=SOCIO5102).
